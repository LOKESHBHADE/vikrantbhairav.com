Share Your Darshan

Have you visited Shri Vikrant Bhairav Ji at the Shipra bank in Ujjain? Share your experience, miracles, and temple photos with devotees worldwide.

Why Share Your Experience?

🙏

Sharing divine experiences strengthens faith for thousands of devotees across the world.

🔥

Every account of Bhairav's grace spreads his glory — it is itself a form of seva (service).

📸

Temple photos and darshan images inspire those who cannot visit sacred sites in person.

🌐

Builds a living archive of modern-day miracles and devotion for future generations.

Submit Your Darshan

Your name is optional — you may share anonymously. All submissions are reviewed before publishing.

॥ जय भैरव नाथ ॥

Your darshan has been received. Lord Bhairav's blessings be upon you and your family.

Share a Temple Photo

Have darshan photos from a Bhairav temple? Send them to us directly by email and we will add them to the gallery with your credit (or anonymously if you prefer).

SEND PHOTOS TO

darshan@vikrantbhairav.com

Include the temple name, location, and your name (if you want credit). Photos should be min 800px wide.

Guidelines for Sharing

  • Share genuine experiences — no fictional stories
  • Be respectful in language; avoid derogatory content about any faith
  • Photos should be of temples, idols, nature, or personal puja setups — not selfies
  • You can share in Hindi or English
  • Experiences of all kinds are welcome — doubt, fear, wonder, and devotion alike
